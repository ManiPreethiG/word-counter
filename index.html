Word Counter

Word Counter is a handy tool to assist you in managing your text documents efficiently. With just a few clicks, you can accurately count the number of words in your text and even analyze the frequency of each word.

How to Use:

Count Total Words: Click on this button to get the total number of words in your text.
Count Word Frequency: Click on this button to analyze the frequency of each word in your text.

Note

If the word contains:
1. Punctuation
2. Hyphens
3. Special Characters
4. Numbers
5. Contractions (e.g., "can't," "won't")
6. URLs/Emails

They are treated as a single word unless you mention them with a white space in between
